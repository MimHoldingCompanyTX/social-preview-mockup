# Photography Brief & Shot List (EN/ES)

Date: 2026-02-20
Owner: Sheila Gutierrez Designs (Uptown Dallas / Preston Hollow / University Park)

## Objective / Objetivo
- EN: Capture elegant, natural-light before/after images that show transformation with minimal purchases.
- ES: Capturar imágenes antes/después con luz natural que muestren transformación con compras mínimas.

## Look & Feel / Estilo
- EN: Timeless, warm neutrals; layered textures; tidy styling; no harsh HDR; vertical-first for IG; avoid wide-angle distortion.
- ES: Atemporal, neutros cálidos; texturas en capas; estilismo ordenado; sin HDR fuerte; vertical para IG; evitar distorsión de gran angular.

## Technical / Técnico
- Camera/Phone: Latest iPhone/Pixel ok; clean lens.
- Formats: RAW or highest-quality JPG/HEIC; keep originals.
- Framing: 2–3 heights (waist, chest, eye); vertical + horizontal.
- Tripod/steady; turn off overheads; use window light; add lamps if needed.

## Styling Kit / Kit de Estilismo
- Throws, pillows, tray, hardcover books, fresh greens, neutral candle, lint roller, microfiber cloth.

## Shot List (Each Room) / Lista de Toma (Cada Habitación)
1) Anchor Wide — BEFORE (room establishing)
2) Hero Wide — AFTER (same angle/framing)
3) Vignette — AFTER (styled corner / console / coffee table)
4) Functional Detail — AFTER (lighting, texture, joinery)
5) Perspective — AFTER (doorway or hallway leading in)
6) Optional People — AFTER (hand placing book/throw; face out-of-frame)

Repeat per room: Living, Entry, Dining, Primary Bed, Guest, Office, Outdoor.

## File Naming / Nombres de Archivo
YYYYMMDD_Room_Angle_BEFORE.jpg  
YYYYMMDD_Room_Angle_AFTER.jpg

## Delivery / Entrega
- Upload originals + selects to Drive/Marketing/Photos/<ProjectName>.
- Include a simple text note: lighting time, challenges, wins.

## Usage / Uso
- Website transformations, Reels (15–20s), carousels, press kit.
