# Den Mood Board Specification (Zillow Photo)

Summary
A cozy, relaxed den with rustic-modern elements: exposed wood beams, neutral upholstery, shiplap accent wall, and built-ins flanking a black-grid slider. The mood is calm, practical, and inviting, with potential for elevated styling via layered textures, curated shelf decor, and warmer accent tones.

NON-NEGOTIABLE FURNITURE RULES (must remain as-is)
- Do not replace, swap, or redesign existing furniture shown in the reference photo.
- Specifically keep: the existing sofa and the existing coffee table (no alternates rendered).
- All styling changes must work with these fixed pieces.

Approximate Color Palette
- Driftwood Beam Brown — #7A5D44
- Sand/Stone Walls — #D9D2C6
- Shiplap Warm White — #F2EFE9
- Sofa Taupe — #BEB4A8
- Charcoal/Black Accents (grid/TV/speakers) — #1E1E1E
- Rug Greige — #C9C1B6

Style Keywords
- Rustic Modern • Transitional • Neutral + Warm Wood • Clean-lined built-ins

What to Keep/Emphasize
- Exposed beams and black window grid as architectural anchors
- Neutral, durable seating with layered pillows/throws for comfort
- Built-in shelving as focal styling zones

Opportunities (Quick Wins)
1) Shelves Styling
   - Mix stacked/books-by-height with negative space.
   - 1–2 small ceramics per bay; one framed photo per column; a low trailing plant on a mid shelf.
   - Keep palettes neutral (bone/stone/black) with one warmer wood accent per side.
2) Textiles
   - Add a subtle-pattern throw (houndstooth/stripe) and swap 1–2 pillows for texture (bouclé/linen).
3) Lighting
   - Introduce a slim black/bronze floor lamp with linen shade near sofa corner.
4) Coffee Table
   - One larger tray + a low bowl; remove small items to reduce clutter.
5) Wall Touch-up
   - If desired, unify wall sheen/patches; keep color in the sand/stone family.

Furniture/Decor Direction
- Materials: natural wood, matte black/bronze, linen, bouclé, stoneware.
- Forms: simple, rectilinear, low profile; avoid glossy chrome.

Cohesion Rules
- Repeat black in 3–4 places (grid, lamp, picture frames, hardware).
- Limit shelf objects to ~5–7 per side; keep at least one open shelf per column.
- Keep wood tones warm and consistent with beams/console; avoid gray woods.

Sourcing Notes (starter list)
- Shelf set: 12–18 neutral hardcover spines, 4–6 matte ceramic pieces (ivory/sand/black), 2–3 natural wood elements, 2 frames (black/wood), one 6–8" low plant.
- Floor lamp: slim, black or oil-rubbed bronze, linen drum/ cone.
- Textiles: 2 pillow covers (linen/bouclé), 1 throw.

Optional Add-Ons & Deliverables
- Shopping List: curated links (Ceramics, frames, tray, bowl, lamp, textiles, books bundle, plant). Primary + budget-friendly alternates.
- Budget Ranges: low/mid/high estimates for styling items (not furniture) with a simple total.
- Source Map: where each item lives on the shelves/room; quick install order of operations.
- Similar Finds Online: 2–3 lookalike options per category to keep the vibe if an item is OOS.
- Installation Plan: 30–60 minute shelf styling checklist + photo angle guidance for before/after.
- Social/Client Assets: IG-ready collage export (1080x1350, 1440x1800) and a one-slide PDF.
