# MARKETING STRATEGY: THE GROK BLUEPRINT

This document summarizes the tactical advice from the Grok marketing session, optimized for Sheila Gutierrez Designs.

---

## 🎯 CORE CHANNELS
1. **Instagram (Priority #1):** The visual portfolio. Best for high-end Dallas visuals and "Before/After" reels.
2. **Facebook:** Best for local community groups and client testimonials.
3. **WhatsApp:** The "Direct Line" for personal network referrals and the Founding 5 campaign.

---

## 💰 ADVERTISING BUDGET (TEST PHASE)
- **Daily Spend:** $10 - $20 to start.
- **Goal:** Focus on **CPC (Cost Per Click)** to drive traffic directly to the website contact form.
- **Targeting:** Focus on Preston Hollow, University Park, and Uptown zip codes. Use interests like "Luxury Living" and "Interior Design."

---

## 📸 AD CREATIVE IDEAS
- **"The Magic Flip":** A split-screen before/after image. Caption: "Your space, reimagined."
- **"The Professional Sanctuary":** A carousel focusing on Uptown condo styling (small space, high elegance).
- **"Expert Tips":** A short video of Sheila explaining one simple change (like lighting or rug sizing).

---

## 📈 KEY PERFORMANCE TERMS
- **CPM (Cost Per Mille):** Cost to show your ad to 1,000 people. Good for "Buzz."
- **CPC (Cost Per Click):** Cost every time someone taps your ad. Good for "Leads."

## 💡 THE "KITCHEN" TIP
Dallas luxury clients respond highest to gold accents, marble, and "moody" kitchen styling. We should prioritize these visuals in our first campaign.
