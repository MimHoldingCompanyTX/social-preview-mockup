### ✅ **TTS Race Condition Fix**
- **Root Cause**: Functions (`stopSpeaking`, `speakText`, `handleSpeakEnglish`, `handleSpeakSpanish`, `translateText`) were being recreated on every render, causing `useEffect` cleanup in `FullScreenViewer` to run prematurely
- **Fix**: Added `useCallback` to all TTS functions with proper dependency arrays:
  - `stopSpeaking` memoized with `[]` (stable reference)
  - `speakText` memoized with `[]` (uses refs, not state)
  - `translateText` memoized with `[]` (no dependencies)
  - `handleSpeakEnglish` memoized with `[previewData, ttsSpeaking, ttsLanguage, stopSpeaking, setDisplayLanguage, translatedContent, setTranslatedContent, speakText]`
