## 💎 POST 1: THE "BIG LIGHT" MISTAKE
**Caption:** "Want to instantly change the mood of your room? Turn off the 'Big Light' (the overhead fixture). Luxury design is all about layers. Add a floor lamp in a dark corner and a small table lamp next to your favorite chair. Warm, low-level lighting makes a space feel like a destination, not just a room. 🐠"

## 💎 POST 2: FINDING YOUR FOCUS
**Caption:** "Every room needs a 'Lead Actor.' Is yours the fireplace? A large window? Or is your furniture all pointing at the TV by default? When I walk into a space, the first thing I do is identify the focal point and make sure everything else in the room supports it. Reveal your room’s true potential by finding its heart. 🐠"

## 💎 POST 3: THE ART OF THE EDIT
**Caption:** "Sometimes, the best thing you can do for a room is take something away. We often hold onto decor that no longer serves the 'Vibe' we want. Curation is about choosing what to keep and what to release so your favorite treasures can finally breathe. What’s one item in your living room you're ready to let go of? 🐠"

## 💎 POST 4: THE "RUG" RULE
**Caption:** "Design Pro Tip: Your rug should be the anchor of your room. A common mistake is buying a rug that is too small. Ideally, all your furniture legs (or at least the front two!) should sit comfortably on the rug. It pulls the pieces together into a unified conversation. 🐠"

## 💎 POST 5: DESIGN FOR YOUR NEW CHAPTER
**Caption:** "Our homes are the backdrop for our lives. If you are entering a new chapter—a new house, a new career, or a fresh start—your space should reflect that growth. Don't just live with your old furniture; curate it to match the person you are becoming today. 🐠"
