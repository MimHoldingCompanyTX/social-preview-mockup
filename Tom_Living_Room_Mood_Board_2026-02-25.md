# Tom's Living Room — Mood Board Specification (Draft)
Date: 2026-02-25
Source: Client photo (living room with lake view)

Summary
A calm, lakeside Transitional space with hints of Coastal and Traditional. Keep the serene neutrals and warm wood; clarify teal as the hero accent and simplify surfaces for an airier, more intentional look.

Proposed Style Keywords
- Lakeside Transitional
- Refined Coastal accents
- Warm wood + calm neutrals + teal accent

Approximate Color Palette
- Lake Teal — #2C6E7C (accent: chairs, pillows, art)
- Sand Beige — #D8CBB6 (existing sofas/walls)
- Warm Taupe — #B9A893 (accent chairs)
- Driftwood Brown — #6B4E3D (floors/beams/tables)
- Soft Ivory — #F4F1EA (rug, sheers, lampshades)
- Oil-Rubbed Bronze / Matte Black — #2E2A27 / #111111 (hardware, lamps)

Key Materials & Finishes
- Textiles: linen, cotton, subtle bouclé; low-contrast patterns
- Woods: medium-warm oak or walnut; avoid greyed woods that fight the floor
- Metals: unify to oil-rubbed bronze or matte black (pick one)
- Glass: maintain sightlines to water; keep table decor minimal

Space Read & Layout Notes (from photo)
- Strengths: abundant natural light; beautiful water view; warm flooring; multiple seating zones.
- Opportunities: area rugs appear undersized/fragmented; surface clutter; mixed metals/finishes create noise; art scale/style not yet unifying.

Recommendations
1) Anchor Rug
- Size up to 9x12 or 10x14 in the main conversation zone (ivory field, subtle pattern). Secondary sitting areas can use smaller neutral flatweaves or go rug-free to reduce visual breaks.

2) Seating & Soft Goods
- Keep overall seating count; reduce small tables. Let teal be the hero via either two accent chairs or a pillow set (not both heavy at once).
- Pillows: mix stripe + small geometric + organic print in teal/ivory/taupe. Limit total patterns to 3 families to reduce busyness.

3) Lighting
- Replace fan light kit with a cleaner profile or low-profile fan. Add two slim bronze/black floor lamps with linen shades to frame the main sofa grouping.
- Dining: consider a fabric or woven drum to soften and echo coastal notes.

4) Windows
- Ripple-fold linen sheers in Soft Ivory (#F4F1EA) to diffuse light while preserving the view. Optional woven wood shades for texture and privacy at night.

5) Art & Styling
- Over-sofa: one larger abstract landscape pulling teal + sand tones.
- Mantle/walls: keep frames consistent (black or warm wood). Style tables with 1-2 larger statements (tray + bowl + greenery) over multiple smalls.

6) Cohesion Rules
- Repeat teal 3–4 times across the room (chairs or pillows, art, one decor object).
- Keep woods in the same warmth family; avoid mixing cool grey woods.
- Unify metals to one finish choice (bronze or black) across lamps, frames, and small hardware.

Quick-Win Action List (next 2 weeks)
- Order larger neutral rug (9x12+)
- Two coordinated floor lamps (slim profile, bronze/black, linen shades)
- Curated pillow set (teal/ivory/taupe, 5–7 pcs total across seating)
- Linen sheers for windows (ripple-fold, ceiling-mounted where possible)
- One large art piece above sofa (lake-inspired palette)
- Edit/declutter table surfaces; add a single live plant for freshness

Optional Sourcing Guide (to prepare if approved)
- Starter / Mid / Premium options for: rug, two lamps, pillow set, art, sheers
- Shoppable links and lead times

Notes
- This draft is based on a single reference photo; dimensions and final selections to be validated on-site or with a quick measurement set.
