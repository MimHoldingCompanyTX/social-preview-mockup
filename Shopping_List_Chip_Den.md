Placement quick guide
- Shelves: 5–7 objects/side, at least 1 open shelf/column. Mix stacked + vertical books; add 1–2 matte ceramics, 1 frame, 1 warm-wood object, 1 trailing plant (mid shelf).
- Coffee table: 1 large tray + 1 low bowl; keep negative space.
- Lamp: slim black/bronze with linen shade at sofa corner to echo window grid.
- Textiles: swap 1–2 pillows for texture (linen/bouclé); add one subtle-pattern throw.
