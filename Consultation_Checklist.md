# CONSULTATION CHECKLIST: THE 2-HOUR SPARK

Sheila, use this list the moment you walk into a client's home. It helps you stay focused and look like the expert.

---

## 📸 BEFORE YOU START
- **Permission:** Ask the client if you can take "Before" photos.
- **The Story:** Ask: "What is the one thing about this room that bothers you most?"

---

## 🔍 THE VISUAL AUDIT
1. **The Focal Point:** What is the first thing eyes hit? (Fireplace? Window? TV?) If it's the TV, can we make it the Fireplace?
2. **The "Anchor" Pieces:** Identify the large furniture that cannot move (Sofas, large rugs).
3. **Lighting Check:** Are there dark corners? Does the room need more lamps instead of "big light" (overhead)?
4. **Traffic Flow:** Can you walk through the room without hitting a corner of a table?
5. **The "Treasures" Inventory:** Look for sentimental items (vases, photos, heirlooms) that are currently "lost" in clutter.

---

## ✍️ THE ACTION PLAN
- **Edit/Remove:** Which pieces of furniture or decor are "fighting" the room's energy?
- **Relocate:** Which items from other rooms would work better here?
- **The "Signature Touch":** What is the one small change (making the bed, fluffing pillows, clearing a surface) that will give an immediate "Aha!" moment?

---

## 💰 THE CLOSE
- **Next Steps:** Mention the "Mood Board" package if they need a deeper design.
- **Billing:** Confirm any extra time spent beyond 2 hours is $100/hr.
